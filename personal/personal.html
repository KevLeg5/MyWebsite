Personal Life

Who am I? Please read and find out more about my upbringing and passions!

My Journey

My name is Kevin Leger. I was born in NYC, but grew up in Tolland Connecticut, a rural town of ~14,000. I am currently 20 years old and stand at 6'2" (188cm). My parents are both first generation immigrants to the United States, my mom from Poland and my dad from Canada. My upbringing has been rooted in family traditions and values from both sides. I strongly value my relationships with family and friends. I have deep passions for sports, music, technology, and gaming, as you will see!

Sports

My love for sports and staying active started before I can remember. I have grown up playing soccer, basketball, skiing, and golf, and more recently picked up tennis and volleyball. I have watched leagues such as the NFL, NBA, CFB, PGA, ATP, and many of the European football leagues. I also consistently workout, and play many of these sports on intramural leagues. Feel free to hit me up to chat about sports!
